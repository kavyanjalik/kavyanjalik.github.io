About

I am doctoral researcher investigating how nationalism is mobilised on digital media networks through the active construction of national identities. My research bridges foundational theories of identity formation on social networks with nationalism and digital communication studies to analyse the micro-level digital interactions and expressions that lead to real-world outcomes, such as voting for radical-right parties. Before pursuing academia in Spain, I worked as a journalist in India with organisations such as Reuters, CNN-News18, and India Today, covering politics, finance and social cultural news, experiences that continue to motivate my current research on media, identity, and political behaviour.
